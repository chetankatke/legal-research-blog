## Summary
This Supreme Court case concerns an appeal by the Rajasthan State Industrial Development and Investment Corporation (RIICO) against a High Court order directing the release of land from acquisition proceedings initiated under the Rajasthan Land Acquisition Act, 1953. The land was originally notified under Section 4(1) in 1979 for industrial development, and possession was taken by the Government before being allotted to a private company for establishing a Gem Industrial Estate.
