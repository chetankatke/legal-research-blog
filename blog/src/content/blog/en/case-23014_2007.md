## Summary
**Summary:**

In *Kailash v. State of M.P.*, Criminal Appeal No. 2260 of 2009, the Supreme Court heard an appeal by the sole accused against the Madhya Pradesh High Court's decision upholding his conviction. The appellant was originally charged under Sections 306 (abetment of suicide) and 376(2)(f) IPC (rape of a minor under 12), but the trial court convicted him only under Section 376(1) IPC, sentencing him to 10 years' rigorous imprisonment. The central factual issue involved the death of a 15-year-old girl (Radha Bai) found hanging inside her house, with the postmortem revealing vaginal injuries (lacerations, congestion, and frozen blood) suggestive of sexual assault, alongside the ligature mark from hanging.
