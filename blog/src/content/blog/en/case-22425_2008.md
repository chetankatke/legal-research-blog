# 22425_2008

## Summary
This appeal arose from a land acquisition dispute where the Punjab Government acquired 68 kanals 3 marlas in Anandpur Sahib for an Urban Estate. The Additional District Judge, Ropar, awarded the respondent (Surinder Kaur) compensation at Rs. 24,200 per marla (Rs. 806.67 per sq. yard) based on sale deed Ex. P/7, which was higher than the Land Acquisition Collector's award. The appellants' revision against this order was not entertained by the Single Judge of the Punjab and Haryana High Court, prompting the appeal before the Supreme Court. The central legal question concerned the maintainability of the revision against the reference court's compensation award, and whether the appellants could challenge the determination of market value through objections under Section 47 CPC in execution proceedings.
