## Summary
**Summary:**

The Supreme Court of India heard an appeal under Section 15Z of the SEBI Act, 1992, filed by N. Narayanan, the promoter and whole-time Director of M/s Pyramid Saimira Theatre Limited (PSTL), challenging a Securities Appellate Tribunal order that upheld SEBI's restraint on him from dealing in securities for two years and a monetary penalty of Rs. 50 lakhs under Section 15HA of the SEBI Act.
