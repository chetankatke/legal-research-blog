## Summary
This appeal before the Supreme Court concerns the interpretation of Section 14 of the Hindu Succession Act, 1956, specifically whether a limited/life interest granted to a female heir under a Will dated 16.9.1944 (executed by Dr. Hira Singh in favor of his daughter Dr. Shivdev Kaur) gets converted into absolute ownership. The trial court, first appellate court, and the High Court of Punjab & Haryana all rejected Dr. Shivdev Kaur's claim of absolute right, holding that she only possessed a life interest in the suit property
