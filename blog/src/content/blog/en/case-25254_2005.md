## Summary
**Summary:**

This case arose from the retrenchment of approximately 256 workmen employed on lift irrigation schemes operated by the Irrigation Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited, a government undertaking established in 1973 in the aftermath of a severe drought. The workmen's services were terminated in 1985 when the irrigation schemes were transferred to a sugar factory, prompting 163 of them to challenge the transfer through a writ petition.
