## Summary
**Summary:**

In this appeal arising under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881, the Supreme Court considered whether the respondent's defence could defeat the cheque-bounce complaint. The respondent had claimed that a prior stop-payment notice dated 28 July 2003, issued in connection with a failed land sale agreement, should have prevented the appellant from acting on the dishonoured cheques.
