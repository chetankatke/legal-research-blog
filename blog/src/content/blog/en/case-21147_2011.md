## Summary
**Summary:**

This batch of criminal appeals before the Supreme Court of India, decided by Justice S.A. Bobde, arises from a common judgment dated 23 March 2004 convicting multiple accused (A-1 to A-20) for the murder of Mudi Parandhami Reddy on 25 November 1996 in an agricultural field at Mettu village, Andhra Pradesh. The prosecution case established that the deceased, a Congress party leader, was attacked by a group of accused led by A-1 Puchalapalli Parandhami Reddy (a Telugu Desam Party leader), who suspected the deceased of being involved in his father's murder in 1992; the attack was also connected to a land dispute over Survey No. 116.
