## Summary
This appeal by U.P. Power Corporation Ltd. arises from a disciplinary proceeding initiated against late Virendra Lal, a retired Assistant Engineer, wherein the UPSEB imposed a 10% deduction from his pension for causing wrongful loss by releasing electricity beyond the approved estimate. The legal questions before the Supreme Court concerned whether there exists statutory authority to recover/deduct from the pension of a retired employee, whether the punishment was validly imposed by the UPSEB rather than the Chairman (as required under Regulation 6(4) of the 1975 Regulations), whether the employee was wrongly deprived of the right of appeal, and whether the disciplinary action violated principles of natural justice and equal treatment. Both the State Public Service Tribunal and the High Court had set aside the punishment order on these grounds.
