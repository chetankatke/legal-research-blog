## Summary
**Summary:**

This case concerns a trademark dispute over the mark "KOHINOOR" in Class 30 (rice) between Satnam Overseas and Sant Ram & Co. The legal question before the Supreme Court centered on the territorial scope of the respondent's
## Key Holdings
- The Assistant Registrar of Trade Marks rightly allowed the rectification application on the ground of non-use of the registered trademark for a period exceeding five years.
- The restriction on the use of the trademark KOHINOOR was initially confined to specific cities in Uttar Pradesh, later extended to the entire state of Uttar Pradesh by the learned Single Judge.
- The Division Bench of the Delhi High Court upheld all the findings of the learned Single Judge by a common order dated 15.05.2007.
- Pending disposal of the appeals, the Division Bench stayed the operation of the Single Judge's order, breach of which led to filing of a contempt application.
