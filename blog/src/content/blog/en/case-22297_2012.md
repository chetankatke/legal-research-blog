## Summary
**Summary:**

In this case, the Supreme Court examined whether the cancellation of the appellants' selection as police constables was justified on the ground that they had suppressed material information regarding their involvement in a criminal case. The appellants, who had participated in a protest (dharna) demanding delayed exam results, were taken to a police station and later signed attestation forms declaring they had no criminal involvement.
