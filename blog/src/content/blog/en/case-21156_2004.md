## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal before the Supreme Court challenged the Jammu & Kashmir High Court's affirmance of the appellant's conviction under Section 302 of the Ranbir Penal Code and life sentence for the murder of Gani Shah. The prosecution alleged that the appellant shot the deceased outside his home after returning from evening prayers, motivated by retaliation for the rejection of his marriage proposal to the deceased's daughter.
