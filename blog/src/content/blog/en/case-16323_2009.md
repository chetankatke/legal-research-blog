## Summary
**Summary:**

This case involves Criminal Appeals filed by Mohinder Singh before the Supreme Court of India challenging the common judgment of the Punjab and Haryana High Court, which confirmed the death sentence imposed by the Sessions Judge, Ludhiana, for the murder of his wife (Veena Verma) and daughter (Geetu Verma). The core legal question is whether the death sentence warrants confirmation, given the aggravating circumstance that the murders were committed in retaliation against the wife who had previously secured the appellant's conviction and 12-year sentence for raping his minor daughter Geetu, thereby eliminating her as a witness.
