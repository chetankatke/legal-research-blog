## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal by the State of Rajasthan arose from the Division Bench of the Rajasthan High Court declining to condone a 98-day delay in filing a Special Appeal Writ challenging a Single Judge's order in eviction proceedings under the Rajasthan Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorized Occupants) Act, 1964. The key legal question was whether the High Court was justified in rejecting the condonation application merely because the State, in its application, inadvertently stated the appeal was filed on 02.
