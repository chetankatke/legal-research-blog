## Summary
In *Rashmi Metaliks Ltd. v. Kolkata Metropolitan Development Authority*, the Supreme Court was called upon to decide whether the Division Bench of the Calcutta High Court was correct in upholding the rejection of the appellant's tender bid for non-compliance with the conditions of the "Invitation to Tender." The tender required, under clauses (i) and (j), a non-blacklisting affidavit and the submission of valid PAN, VAT, and Income Tax/Professional Tax returns, which the appellant had failed to furnish comprehensively. The Division Bench, relying on *W.B. State Electricity Board v. Patel Engineering Co. Ltd.*, held that the appellant was ineligible for consideration of its bid.
