## Summary
This appeal before the Supreme Court (Justice Anil R. Dave) arose from a common judgment of the Bombay High Court dismissing First Appeals Nos. 492, 493, and 499 of 2002, where the original plaintiffs had sought specific performance of agreements to purchase flats. The plaintiffs had entered into negotiations with a Developer (who had a development agreement with M/s. O.P. Co-operative Housing Society, the landowner) and paid Rs. 88,000/- each as initial installments pursuant to a "letter of intent" dated 29th September 1992, which reserved flats for them
