## Summary
In this appeal, the Supreme Court addressed whether the Orissa High Court erred in allowing a writ petition challenging BPCL's re-advertisement of an LPG dealership location under the open category after the original SC-reserved vacancy went unfilled. Respondent No. 5, an SC candidate, had been found ineligible in the first round because he did not possess the required land document as of the cutoff date of 20.11.2009, and no other candidate was found suitable. The Supreme Court set aside the High Court's order, holding that the High Court erred in granting the writ because eligibility had to be determined as of the last date for submitting applications, and Respondent No. 5 admittedly did not own the required parcel of land at the dealership location on that date.
