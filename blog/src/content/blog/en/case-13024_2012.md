## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal arises from three successive eviction suits (R.A.E. Suits Nos. 1103/1976, 1104/1977, and 173/256 of 2010) filed by the same landlords against the same tenant concerning the same premises ("Hanoo Manor") in Mumbai, each founded on different grounds (bona fide requirement, non-user, and subsequent non-user). The legal question before the Supreme Court was whether the third suit was liable to be stayed pending disposal of the first two earlier suits, where the parties, subject matter, and issues were substantially identical.
