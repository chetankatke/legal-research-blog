## Summary
**Summary:**

This Supreme Court criminal appeal (No. 1738 of 2007) arose from a dowry
## Key Holdings
- A1-Babu was convicted under Section 498A IPC for subjecting the deceased to cruelty in connection with dowry demands and under Section 304 Part I IPC for the resulting death.
- A2-Pappathi was convicted under Section 498A IPC for cruelty and under Section 304 Part I read with Section 109 IPC for abetting the offence of causing the deceased's death.
- The Madras High Court confirmed the conviction and sentence of both appellants, leading to the present appeal by special leave.
- Persistent and escalating dowry demands, coupled with harassment of the deceased by the husband and his sister, established the offences of cruelty and abetment to murder.
