## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal arises from a motor accident claim where the injured, a 17-year-old who suffered 80% permanent disability and became bedridden requiring continuous nursing, was initially awarded Rs. 18,75,800/- by the Motor Accident Claims Tribunal. The Andhra Pradesh High Court, on appeal by the insurer, reduced the compensation to Rs. 12,45,800/-. The parents of the injured challenged this reduction before the Supreme Court, contending that the Tribunal's award was just and proper given the severity of the disability and the neurologist's testimony that no improvement in the injured's health was possible. The legal question before the Court is whether the High Court erred in reducing the compensation awarded to a severely disabled young victim of a motor accident.
