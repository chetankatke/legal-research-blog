# 21441_2012

## Summary
This Supreme Court appeal challenged the Gujarat High Court's decision converting the appellant's conviction from Section 304 Part-I IPC (culpable homicide not amounting to murder) to Section 302 IPC (murder), along with the dismissal of his appeal against conviction. The case arose from an incident where the appellant, after a prior dispute with the deceased's brother, allegedly hit the deceased's bicycle from behind with a motor vehicle, tied him behind the vehicle, and dragged him approximately 10 kilometers before abandoning the body. The trial court had convicted the appellant under Section 304 Part-I IPC (punishable up to 10 years), but the High Court enhanced the conviction to Section 302 IPC (life imprisonment) based on the State's appeal. The Supreme Court was called upon to determine whether the High Court erred in altering the conviction from culpable homicide to murder given the nature of the act and the prosecution's evidence.
