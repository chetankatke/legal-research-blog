## Summary
This writ petition under Article 32 was filed by Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) volunteers seeking a Special Investigation Team inquiry into alleged police atrocities at Gokul Puri Police Station in Delhi on 19.06.2013, where they claimed to have been brutally beaten and arrested while peacefully protesting the police's refusal to register an FIR in a rape case.
