## Summary
This is a criminal appeal by Atmaram challenging the Bombay High Court's judgment upholding his conviction under Sections 306 (abetment of suicide) and 498A (cruelty by husband or relatives) of the Indian Penal Code, along with that of his first wife and parents. The prosecution's case was that the appellant's second wife Purnabai was harassed and denied food after delivering a female child, ultimately driving her to jump into a well with her daughter and commit suicide. The Sessions Court had relied on the presumption of abetment under Section 113A of the Indian Evidence Act, 1872
