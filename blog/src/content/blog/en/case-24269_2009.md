In Criminal Appeal No. 43 of 2010, the Supreme Court of India (per V. Gopala Gowda, J.) heard Umesh Singh's challenge to the Bihar High Court's judgment affirming his conviction under Section 302 read with Section 34 IPC and Section 27 of the Arms Act, along with co-accused for the murder of Shailendra Kumar on 16.07.
