## Summary
The Supreme Court heard appeals concerning the interpretation of Section 173-A of the Rajasthan Municipalities Act, 1959 (as amended by Act No. 19 of 1999), which deals with the State Government's power to permit change in land use upon payment of conversion charges. The Rajasthan High Court, following the Supreme Court's earlier decision in *State of Rajasthan v. Pareshar Soni* (2007) 14 SCC 144, held that the Municipal Corporation is not empowered to demand any amount for change of land use, thereby quashing the demand notice issued to the respondents who had applied for conversion of their residential plot to commercial use.
