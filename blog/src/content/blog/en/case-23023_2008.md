## Summary
In this appeal, the Supreme Court of India is reviewing a judgment of the High Court of Punjab & Haryana that reversed the acquittal of the appellant, Prem Singh, and another accused (Vishwa Bandhu) in a murder case. The case arose from an incident on 26.11.1993, in which one Siri Krishan was shot dead by persons who arrived in a Maruti car while he was on a morning walk; the prosecution alleged the involvement of seven accused, with recoveries of firearms and empty cartridges made during investigation. While the Trial Court had acquitted all the accused, the High Court convicted Prem Singh and Vishwa Bandhu under Section 302 read with Section 34 IPC, sentencing them to life imprisonment.
