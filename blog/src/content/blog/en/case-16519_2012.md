## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal arises from a challenge by the appellant and her two sisters to a 1988 sale deed executed by their mother (second respondent) over ancestral property of their deceased father, Khilluram. The daughters contended that since they held equal shares in the property and were minors at the time, their mother had no authority to sell the property without prior permission of the court. Both the trial court and the High Court rejected the suit, finding no illegality in the sale, with the mother claiming the sale proceeds were used for the daughters' needs. The Supreme Court (per Justice S. Jyoti Mukhopadhaya) granted leave to appeal against the High Court's decision affirming the dismissal of the suit seeking cancellation of the sale deed.
