**Summary:**

In *Gurdip Singh v. State of Punjab*, the Supreme Court considered an appeal by an elderly father-in-law challenging his conviction under Sections 498A and 304B of the IPC for the alleged dowry death of his daughter-in-law, Harjit Kaur, who was found dead under suspicious circumstances in 1990 after repeated demands for a scooter, refrigerator, and cash as dowry.
