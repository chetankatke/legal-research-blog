**Summary:**

This appeal arises from a challenge to the 2000 amendments to the Maharshi Mahesh Yogi Vedic Vishwavidyalaya Adhiniyam, 1995, which
## Key Holdings
- The amendment to Section 4(1) of the 1995 Act was upheld as intra-vires.
- The amendments to Sections 9(2), 31-A(1) and (2), 31-B, 31-C, 37-B(a), 37-B(b), 37-B(d) and 37-B(e) were held to be intra-vires the Constitution.
- The proviso to Section 4 requiring prior State Government approval for establishing new Centres is intra-vires, but does not apply to existing Centres.
- The proviso to Section 4 mandating prior State Government approval for conducting courses is ultra-vires insofar as it relates to existing courses and existing Centres.
- Section 37-A, inserted by the Amendment Act, was held to be ultra-vires in its entirety.
