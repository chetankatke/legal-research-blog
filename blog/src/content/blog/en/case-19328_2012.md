## Summary
This case concerns the selection process for the Rajasthan Judicial Services, where the Supreme Court had previously directed that all candidates who cleared the preliminary examination—under both the original and revised result declarations—be permitted to appear in the written (Main) examination, and requested the Rajasthan High Court to conduct the examination.
