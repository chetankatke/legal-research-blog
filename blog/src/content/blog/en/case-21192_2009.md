## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal arises from a dispute over a 2,500 sq. mtr. portion of land owned by Respondent No. 1, which was reserved in the 1997 Development Plan for the extension of Royal College of Arts, Science and Commerce. The Corporation initiated acquisition proceedings in December 2005, and a Division Bench of the High Court directed the Special Land Acquisition Officer to pass an award within six months—a deadline that was not met.
