## Summary
**Summary:**

In this appeal arising from a discharge order, the Supreme Court (per Vikramajit Sen, J.) was called upon to determine whether criminal proceedings under Sections 302 and 498-A read with Section 34 IPC could continue against the parents of a deceased husband, after the husband (the primary accused) had committed suicide. The case arose from the murder of Sujatha, who was stabbed to death by her husband Ramachandra Reddy in a Pondicherry hotel room, allegedly in furtherance of a common intention with his parents to extort dowry. The Trial Court had allowed the parents' discharge petition under Section 227 CrPC, and the High Court declined to interfere in revision.
