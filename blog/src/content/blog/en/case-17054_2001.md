## Summary
**Summary:**

These two criminal appeals, filed under Section 10 of the Special Court (Trial of Offences Relating to Transactions in Securities) Act, 1992, arise from the conviction of accused Nos. 1 and 3 by the Special Court in a case prosecuted by the CBI. The prosecution alleged that accused No. 3 (Hiten P. Dalal), an approved broker of Canbank Mutual Fund (CMF), fraudulently claimed brokerage on transactions worth Rs. 33 crores (involving Andhra Bank and ABFSL) and Rs. 32 crores (involving Sahara India and IDBI) by falsely representing himself as the procuring broker, even though he was not appointed as such and the consideration was paid by him.
