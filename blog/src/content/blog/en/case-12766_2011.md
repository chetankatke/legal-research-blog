## Summary
**Summary:** This Supreme Court appeal between ONGC Ltd. and M/s. Modern Construction and Co. concerns whether a decree-holder is entitled to interest for the period during which its suit was pending before a court later held to lack territorial jurisdiction. The respondent had filed suits in 1986 to recover outstanding dues, which were ultimately decreed in 2006 by the Surat court (the court of competent jurisdiction), with interest running from the date of refiling (1999).
