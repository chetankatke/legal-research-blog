## Summary
**Summary:**

This batch of civil appeals before the Supreme Court concerned whether an assessee could be denied deemed MODVAT credit on inputs purchased from a manufacturer who had not discharged the appropriate excise duty on those goods. The respondent, M/s. Kay Kay Industries, had availed deemed MODVAT credit of Rs. 77,546/- based on invoices issued by a steel re-rolling mill, but verification revealed that the supplier had not paid the full duty liability for the relevant period.
