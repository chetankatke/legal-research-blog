## Summary
In **Aneesh D. Lawande & others v. State of Goa & others (Writ Petition (C) No. 598 of 2013)**, the Supreme Court of India addressed a writ petition concerning irregularities in postgraduate (PG) medical course admissions at the lone Government medical college in Goa.
