## Summary
**Summary:**

In this criminal appeal, the Supreme Court of India examined whether the Delhi High Court was justified in quashing prosecution proceedings against officers of the Directorate General of Central Excise Intelligence (DGCEI) for allegedly illegally detaining and assaulting the appellant (a director of M/s. Vinay Wires) in connection with excise duty evasion proceedings. The Magistrate had taken cognizance of offenses under Sections 323, 348, 365, 368, 506 read with 34 and 120-B IPC based on a Medico-Legal Case Report confirming multiple injuries on the complainant's body.
