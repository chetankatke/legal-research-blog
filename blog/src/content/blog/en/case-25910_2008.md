## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal by the State of Bihar challenges the Patna High Court's order in a PIL directing the Chief Secretary to implement within one month the commitments made to the Bihar State University and College Employees Federation regarding parity between non-teaching staff of Universities/Constituent Colleges and State Government employees. The core legal question is whether the High Court could issue a mandamus compelling the State to honour successive agreements/understandings (dated 2003, 2005, and 2007) granting pay and service-condition parity, which had remained unimplemented despite repeated strikes and assurances.
