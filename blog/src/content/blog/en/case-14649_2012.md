## Summary
This Supreme Court appeal concerns whether the Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai validly sanctioned the change of land use of property situated in a Special Industrial Zone (I-3 Zone) to permit construction of a residential and commercial complex by Oswal Agro Mills Ltd. The legal question before the Court was whether the Bombay High Court rightly quashed the change of user sanction given the security objections raised by Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Ltd. (HPCL), which had acquired adjacent land for petroleum storage tanks, along with concerns from the Police Department, Ministry of Petroleum, Ministry of Environment, and Intelligence Bureau under the Security Control Regulations framed under Section 37(1AA) of the Maharashtra Regional and Town Planning Act, 1966.
