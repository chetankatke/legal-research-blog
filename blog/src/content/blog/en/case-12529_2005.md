## Summary
This case concerns the jurisdiction over workmen's claims when recoveries are pursued under certificates issued by the Debts Recovery Tribunal (DRT) against companies in liquidation. The core legal question is whether such workmen's claims for *pari passu* payment must be adjudicated by the official liquidator or by the DRT, an issue the Court considered important enough to refer to a three-Judge Bench due to its implications for numerous pending cases. The dispute arose from a writ petition by workmen/employees of Paper and Pulp Conversions Ltd. (ordered wound up by BIFR in 1993) seeking directions to the DRT's Recovery Officer to recover Rs. 3 crores
