## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal arises from the conviction of Shimbhu and Balu Ram for gang rape (Section 376(2)(g) IPC) and related offences, after they abducted a young woman at knifepoint in December 1995 in Haryana, confined her in a shop for two days, and repeatedly raped her. Both the Additional Sessions Judge, Narnaul, and the High Court of Punjab and Haryana upheld the conviction, imposing rigorous imprisonment of ten years under Section 376(2)(g) read with Section 34 IPC, along with concurrent sentences for kidnapping, wrongful confinement, and criminal intimidation. The appellants challenged the conviction before the Supreme Court, raising issues regarding the credibility of the prosecution's evidence and the reliability of the prosecutrix's testimony.
