## Summary
This Supreme Court appeal arose from a land acquisition dispute where approximately 7 bighas of land were acquired under Section 4 read with Section 17 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894, for constructing a sector road under the Bahadurgarh Scheme. The legal question centered on whether the compensation awarded by the Land Acquisition Collector—Rs. 3,00,000 per acre for irrigated land and Rs. 1.5 lakh per acre for waste (gair mumkin) land—was adequate
## Key Holdings
- Where land acquired under a single notification includes both irrigated and waste land with residential and commercial potential, uniform compensation may be awarded for both categories of land.
