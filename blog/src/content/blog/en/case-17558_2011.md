## Summary
**Summary:**

In *Madhu @ Madhuranatha & Anr. v. State of Karnataka*, the Supreme Court addressed criminal appeals challenging the High Court of Karnataka's judgment affirming the death sentence imposed by the Sessions Court. The appellants were convicted under Sections 364 (kidnapping), 302 (murder), and 201 (causing disappearance of evidence) read with Section 34 IPC, based on the kidnapping and murder of Madhusudhan, who was sent to collect outstanding dues and was last seen in the company of the appellants carrying the collected money.
