## Summary
**Summary:**

The Supreme Court addressed whether the first proviso to Section 125(3) of the CrPC bars a claimant from recovering arrears of maintenance for a period beyond one year preceding the date of the application. The High Court had interpreted the proviso as a limitation on substantive entitlement, restricting the wife and son's claim for arrears to only one year prior to filing.
