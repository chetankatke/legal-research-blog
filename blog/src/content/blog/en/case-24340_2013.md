## Summary
This writ petition under Article 32 of the Constitution challenged AIIMS' MBBS admission process for 2013, alleging that the institute had exceeded the legally permissible 50% ceiling on reservations by allotting more seats to SC, ST, and OBC candidates than the prescribed quotas (7.5% ST, 15% SC, and 27% OBC). The petitioners contended that the methodology of selecting reserved category candidates—particularly the "carry forward" rule whereby SC/ST/OBC candidates selected on the same standard as general candidates are not adjusted against reserved vacancies—resulted in total reservations exceeding 50%, contrary to Supreme Court precedents. AIIMS defended its admission procedure as valid and in compliance with the law.
