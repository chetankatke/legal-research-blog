## Summary
**Summary:**

This case involves an arbitration petition filed by Schlumberger Asia Services Ltd. (a Hong Kong company) against Oil & Natural Gas Corporation Ltd. (ONGC) under Section 11(6) of the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, seeking appointment of a respondent-nominated arbitrator and a presiding arbitrator for an Arbitral Tribunal to adjudicate disputes arising from a contract dated December 7, 2004. The central legal issue concerns ONGC's preliminary objection to the maintainability of the petition, alleging abuse of process and that the claims are time-barred ("dead claims").
