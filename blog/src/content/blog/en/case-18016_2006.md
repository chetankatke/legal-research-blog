## Summary
**Summary:**

This Supreme Court appeal in *Bhanwar Lal & Anr. vs. Rajasthan Board of Muslim Wakf & Ors.* raises the jurisdictional question of whether a civil suit involving wakf property should be heard by the Civil Court or lies exclusively within the domain of the Tribunal constituted under Section 85 of the Rajasthan Wakf Act, 1995. The Rajasthan Board of Muslim Wakf had filed a civil suit in 1980 for possession and rendition of accounts over a property in Nagaur claimed to be wakf property, later amending the plaint to challenge a sale deed executed by a trustee. The Civil Court returned the plaint on the Board's own application citing the jurisdictional bar under Section 85, and the High Court dismissed the defendants' revision petition, prompting the present appeal.
