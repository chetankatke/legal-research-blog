## Key Holdings
- Information sought relating to the Annual Confidential Report of a third party constitutes 'third party information' attracting the exemption under Section 8(1)(j) of the Right to Information Act, 2005.
- When third party information is requested, the third party is entitled to plead a privacy defence against disclosure.
- The decisive question for disclosure of third party information is whether divulging such information is in the larger public interest.
- Before any disclosure of third party information, the procedure prescribed under Section 11 of the Right to Information Act must be strictly followed.
