## Summary
This appeal before the Supreme Court of India challenges the appellant's conviction under Section 3(1)(c) of the Official Secrets Act, 1923, arising from allegations that he supplied secret information to Pakistani Intelligence operatives, for which he was sentenced to seven years' rigorous imprisonment (a conviction confirmed by the Rajasthan High Court). The principal legal questions raised concern the reliability of the prosecution's evidence—specifically, the recovery of a trace map (Ex.D-3) from the appellant's house, the disclosures made by co-accused Mohd.
