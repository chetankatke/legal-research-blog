## Summary
**Summary:**

This is a criminal appeal under Section 379 CrPC filed by Joginder Singh challenging the High Court of Punjab and Haryana's judgment that partly reversed the trial court's acquittal and convicted him under Section 302 IPC, sentencing him to life imprisonment. The case arose from a land dispute over a shamlat plot assigned to Guru Ravidass Mandir, where on 15.11.1991, an altercation over parking a combine harvester escalated into violence, with the appellant allegedly firing a DBBL .12 bore gun, causing the death of Kamla and injuries to several others.
