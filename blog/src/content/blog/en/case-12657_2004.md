## Summary
This Supreme Court appeal by the State of Chennai challenged the Madras High Court's order quashing an FIR registered against the respondent under Section 120B IPC and Sections 420/467/468/471 IPC read with the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988. The central legal question was whether the High Court was justified in quashing the FIR under Section 482 CrPC solely
