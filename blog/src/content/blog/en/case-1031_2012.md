# Court in Nair Service Society v. District Officer, Kerala Public Service Commission

## Summary
This Supreme Court appeal by the Kerala Public Service Commission (KPSC) challenged the Kerala High Court's Division Bench direction to operate the supplementary list after the main rank list had been exhausted, in order to fill a vacancy arising from the non-joining of a candidate in the Ezhava reserved category. The core legal question was whether, once the main list is exhausted, the supplementary list retains any "life" to fill subsequent vacancies, particularly in reservation quotas. KPSC relied on the Supreme Court's earlier precedent in *Nair Service Society v. District Officer, Kerala Public Service Commission*
