## Summary
**Summary:**

This is a criminal appeal before the Supreme Court of India challenging the conviction of Karan Singh under Section 302 IPC for the murder of one Raj, with both the Trial Court and the High Court of Punjab & Haryana having upheld the conviction and life sentence.
