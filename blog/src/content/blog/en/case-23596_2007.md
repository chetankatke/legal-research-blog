## Summary
This is a criminal appeal before the Supreme Court challenging the Punjab and Haryana High Court's judgment convicting the appellant (accused no. 4) under Section 304B IPC (dowry death) and sentencing him to 7 years' rigorous imprisonment, while reversing the trial court's acquittal. The prosecution alleged that the appellant and his family members (his father, mother, and brother) demanded a scooter and refrigerator as dowry from the deceased, Karnail Kaur, who was found dead on 30/06/1991. While the trial court acquitted all accused for insufficient evidence, the High Court convicted only the appellant, giving the benefit of doubt to the other co-accused (accused no. 1 having died).
