## Summary
The Supreme Court of India heard two connected civil appeals challenging a Division Bench judgment of the Orissa High Court (dated 28.3.2012 in WP(C) No. 23268 of 2011), which had quashed the appointment of the 5th respondent and directed the Central Electricity Supply Utility of Odisha (CESU) to recover honorarium paid to him, pursuant to a public interest litigation filed by the first respondent. The legal question centers on the validity of an appointment made by CESU—a deemed licensee under Section 22 of the Electricity Act, 2003, established through the successive restructuring of the Orissa electricity sector (from OSEB to GRIDCO, and then to distribution companies like CESCO/CESU).
