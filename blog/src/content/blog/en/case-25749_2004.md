## Summary
**Summary:**

This case arises from a motor vehicle accident in which Puran Singh, the owner of an insured jeep, died when the vehicle overturned on 22nd February 1995, with one Chandan Singh driving at the time. His legal heirs filed a claim for compensation under Section 166 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 against New India Assurance Company, which denied liability based on the policy's standard coverage clause and the IMT 12 endorsement regarding legal liability to passengers. The central legal question is whether the insurance policy covered liability for the death of the vehicle's owner himself while being carried in his own vehicle, given the terms of the policy.
