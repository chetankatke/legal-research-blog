## Summary
**Summary:**

This Supreme Court appeal arose from a dispute over transmission schemes notified by the Orissa State Electricity Board (predecessor of the appellant, Orissa Power Transmission Corporation Limited) under Section 29 of the Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, with the original notification issued on May 30, 1991 (published July 20, 1991) and certain schemes modified via a subsequent notification dated January 30, 1996. The respondents, including the Asian School of Business Management Trust, were affected parties (likely landowners) challenging the acquisition or implementation of these transmission schemes. The case turned on the procedural validity and legal effect of the Section 29 notifications
