## Summary
This case concerns a writ petition challenging the constitutional validity of Chapter VIII-A of the Maharashtra Housing and Area Development Act, 1976, which was introduced in 1986 to enable the acquisition of "cessed properties" for cooperative housing societies of occupiers, with the subject matter being the acquisition of "Chhotalal Niwas" in Mumbai by MHADA. The petition, originally tagged with another case raising identical issues, had been referred first to a 7-Judge Bench and then to a 9-Judge Bench for final decision, but no final decision was forthcoming for over a decade.
