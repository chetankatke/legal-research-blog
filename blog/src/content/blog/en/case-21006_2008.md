## Summary
This Supreme Court case addressed whether assessees were liable to pay interest under Section 112 of the Finance Act, 2000 in connection with Central Excise Rule violations. The respondent, a yarn and fabric manufacturer, had availed MODVAT credit on the excise duty paid for High Speed Diesel Oil (HSD Oil) used as fuel for electricity generation in manufacturing, despite a 1995 Notification and 1997 Trade Notice that had withdrawn this credit facility under Rules 57A and 57B. The core legal question concerned the interest liability arising from the wrongful claim of MODVAT credit that was no longer permissible under the law.
