## Summary
**Summary:**

This civil appeal arises from a partition suit concerning properties of the late Shri Kunnaiah, where the central legal question is whether the suit schedule properties were his self-acquired assets (as claimed by the plaintiff and the appellant) or ancestral joint family properties (as contended by defendants 1, 2, and 4). The trial court held the properties were self-acquired and accepted the validity of a Will dated 14 June 1991 bequeathing 32 acres 55 cents to the appellant (defendant No. 3), thereby denying the plaintiff's claim.
