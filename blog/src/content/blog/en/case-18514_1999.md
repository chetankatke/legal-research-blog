# 18514_1999

## Citation

- **Date:** 28.2.1983

## Summary
This appeal by a landlord against the Karnataka High Court's order concerned the question of from which date interest on compensation was payable when his land vested with the State under Section 44 of the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (as amended in 1974). The compensation of Rs. 17,244 was determined in 1983 and paid in cash between 1983-1985 without interest, prompting the landlord to seek interest at 5½% per annum w.e.f. 1st March 1974. The High Court had granted interest only from 1st March 1984, rejecting the claim for interest from the date of vesting. The Supreme Court was called upon to determine whether the landlord was entitled to interest from the date the land vested with the State (1974) or only from the later date fixed by the High Court (1984).
