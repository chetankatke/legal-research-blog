## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal before the Supreme Court of India arose from a CBI prosecution for offences under the Indian Penal Code and the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988, where the appellant-accused sought to examine three additional defence witnesses under Section 311 Cr.P.C. after closing her defence. The legal question was whether the Trial Court and High Court erred in rejecting this application, given that the appellant had moved promptly after co-accused concluded their defence, had provided reasons for seeking the examination, and the case had already spanned over a decade.
