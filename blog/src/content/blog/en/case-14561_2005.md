## Summary
This is an order of the Supreme Court of India disposing of a Contempt Petition (Civil) No. 172 of 2010, which had been filed in connection with Writ Petition (Civil) No. 354 of 2005. The court held that since the underlying writ petition had already been dismissed, there was nothing left for consideration in the contempt petition, as it could not survive independently. Accordingly, the contempt petition was disposed of in light of the dismissal of the main writ petition. The order was passed on September 9, 2013 by Justices H.L. Dattu and M.Y. Eqbal.
## Key Holdings
- The dismissal of Writ Petition (Civil) No.354 of 2005 left nothing for further consideration in the connected Contempt Petition.
