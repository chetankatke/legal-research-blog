**Summary:**

This contempt petition arose from allegations that Respondent No. 1 (Tuncay Alankus) withdrew a substantial sum of money from a Swiss bank account in violation of the Supreme Court's orders dated September 4, 2006 and December 14, 2006. On April 1, 2010, the Court found him guilty of contempt based on his failure to deny the withdrawal allegation in his reply, though sentencing was deferred to allow him to deposit the withdrawn amount.
