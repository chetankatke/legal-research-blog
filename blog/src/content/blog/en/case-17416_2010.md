## Summary
This appeal arises from the conviction of Sujit Biswas under Sections 376(2)(f) and 302 of the Indian Penal Code for the rape and murder of a 3-year-old girl, Sima Khatoon, on 17.10.2007, after abducting her during a Durga Pooja celebration in Guwahati. The Sessions Court imposed the death sentence, which the High Court of Guwahati commuted to life imprisonment with a direction that the appellant would not be entitled to any remission under Sections 432 and 433 of the Cr.P.C. The matter was before the Supreme Court to determine whether the death sentence warranted restoration. The case engages the consideration of the "rarest of rare" doctrine and the proportionality of sentencing in cases involving the rape and murder of a minor child below 12 years of age.
