## Summary
This case concerns appeals challenging a High Court order that set aside a trial court's permission to amend the written statement in a property dispute between M/s Future Builders Co-operative Housing Society (plaintiff) and its promoters, including S. Malla Reddy and family members. The plaintiff-Society alleged that the defendants, who held the sale deed and patta in their own names, were merely trustees holding the suit property for the benefit of the Society pursuant to agreements dated 8.3.1978 and 16.09.1981. The key legal question before the Supreme Court was whether the High Court rightly exercised its supervisory jurisdiction under Article 227 to interfere with the trial court's discretionary order allowing the amendment.
