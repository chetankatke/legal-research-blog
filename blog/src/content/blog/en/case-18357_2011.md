## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal arises from a dispute where U.P. Avas Evam Vikas Parishad conducted an auction in 1977 for a Cinema Hall plot in Bareilly, which the respondent (Om Prakash Sharma) won as the highest bidder at Rs. 1,31,500/- by depositing 20% of the bid amount plus earnest money. The Housing Commissioner subsequently rejected the bid and refunded the deposit, prompting the respondent to file a suit that was decreed in his favor by the trial court. The High Court of Allahabad allowed the second appeal in favor of the respondent, framing and answering substantial questions of law in his favor, which is now challenged before the Supreme Court.
