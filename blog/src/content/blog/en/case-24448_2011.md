In *Noor Mohammed v. Jethanand*, the Supreme Court (Justice Dipak Misra) addressed a Special Leave Petition arising from a civil suit (No. 42 of 1990) filed in Rajasthan for injunctive relief regarding suit land, in which the defendant had filed a counter-claim.
