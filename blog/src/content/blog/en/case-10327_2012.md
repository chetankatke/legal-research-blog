## Summary
**Summary:**

This batch of criminal appeals before the Supreme Court of India concerns complaints filed by Gujarat State Fertilizer Co. Ltd. against Esslon Synthetics Ltd. and its directors—including A.K. Singhania (accused No. 7) and Vikram Prakash (accused No. 9)—alleging offenses under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act. The core legal question involves the liability of directors and officers of a company for cheque dishonor offenses, particularly when they are alleged to be responsible for the company's financial affairs and business administration. The complaints alleged that the directors collectively managed, organized, and administered the company's business and financial transactions, thereby making them vicariously responsible for the issuance of dishonored cheques.
