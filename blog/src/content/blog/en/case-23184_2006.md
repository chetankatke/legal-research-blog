## Summary
In this brief order from the Supreme Court of India, the applicants (Project Director, Lok Jambish Parishad & Anr.) sought permission to withdraw Interlocutory Application No. 1 of 2011, which had been filed in I.A. No. 5 of 2010 within Civil Appeal No. 4059 of 2007 against Zebunissha & Ors. The court, presided over by Justices H.L. Dattu and Chandramauli Kr. Prasad, granted the permission sought by the applicants' counsel. Consequently, I.A. No. 1 of 2011 was dismissed as withdrawn. The order was passed on January 4, 2013, in New Delhi.
