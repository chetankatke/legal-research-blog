## Summary
**Summary:**

This Civil Appeal before the Supreme Court arose from a dispute over land in village Kilokri, Delhi, where the appellants (successors of Bhimandas Ambwani) had title to land acquired by the Government without payment of compensation. The legal question concerned the validity of supplementary award No. 1651-A dated 16.2.1974, made with reference to a Section 4 notification dated 5.3.1963, and whether a subsequent Section 4 notification dated 7.10.1968 had superseded the earlier acquisition proceedings,
