**Summary:**

This appeal by the State of Haryana challenges a High Court judgment concerning the cancellation of a Sales Tax Exemption Certificate granted to M/S Polar Industries Ltd. under Section 13-B of the Haryana General Sales Tax Act, 1973 and Rule 28-B.
