## Summary
**Summary:**

In *Indra Sarma v. V.K.V. Sarma* (Criminal Appeal No. 2009 of 2013), the Supreme Court addressed whether a "live-in relationship" qualifies as a "relationship in the nature of marriage" under Section 2(f) of the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005, thereby entitling the woman to protection against "domestic violence" under Section 3. The appellant, who had lived with the married respondent for several years in a shared household, alleged abandonment and financial exploitation after the relationship broke down.
