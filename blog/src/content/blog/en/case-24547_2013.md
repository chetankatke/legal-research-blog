## Summary
This Supreme Court appeal arose from a Punjab & Haryana High Court order in a review application, which clarified that Rural Medical Officers (RMOs/in-service candidates) could compete in the 40% general category seats with weightage for their tenure of service, in addition to their separate 60% in-service quota, for MD course admissions.
