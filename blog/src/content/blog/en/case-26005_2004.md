## Summary
**Summary:**

This Supreme Court of India reference arose from a referral order directing that the judgment in *Saketh India Ltd. & Ors. v. India Securities Ltd.* (1999) 3 SCC 1 be reconsidered by a larger Bench. The core legal question concerns the interpretation of proviso (c) to Section 138 and Section 142(b) of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881—specifically, whether the one-month limitation period for filing a complaint under Section 138 is to be computed by excluding the date on which the cause of action arose. The Court noted a variance between the view expressed in *Saketh* and the earlier decision in *SIL Import, USA v. Exim Aides Silk Exporters*, Bangalore (1999) 4 SCC 567, necessitating reconsideration.
