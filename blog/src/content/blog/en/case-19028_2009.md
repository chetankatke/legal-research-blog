## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal arises from a suit for specific performance of an agreement dated 29.05.1986, wherein M/s Nanak Builders (Respondent No. 1) sought to enforce a contract for the sale of premises in Connaught Place, New Delhi, against the Sawhney family (original owners). The appellant, Thomson Press (India) Ltd., sought impleadment as a defendant under Order 1 Rule 10 of the CPC, claiming a subsequent interest in the suit property, but both the Single Judge and the Division Bench of the Delhi High Court rejected this application
