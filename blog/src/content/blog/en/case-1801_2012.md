## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal arises from a land dispute between the appellant (Ravinder Singh) and respondent no. 1 (Sukhbir Singh), where the appellant sought to quash criminal proceedings initiated against him under Section 3(1)(viii) of the Scheduled Castes & Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989, following multiple cross-FIRs and a series of failed attempts by respondent no. 1 to forcibly possess the appellant's agricultural land in village Nangli Poona, Delhi. The Delhi High Court dismissed the appellant's application for quashing, prompting the appeal before the Supreme Court.
