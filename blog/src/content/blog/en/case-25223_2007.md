## Summary
In this criminal appeal, the State of Rajasthan challenged the Rajasthan High Court's judgment that modified the conviction of Girdhari Lal from Section 304B IPC (dowry death) to Section 306 IPC (abetment to suicide) in connection with the death of his wife Babita, who died by burning within four years of marriage. The State argued that since the deceased died under unnatural circumstances within seven years of marriage, the burden shifted to the respondent to prove his innocence, warranting conviction under Section 304B IPC rather than the lesser offense. The trial court had originally convicted the respondent under Section 304B IPC and sentenced him to life imprisonment, but the High Court reduced this to five years rigorous imprisonment under Section 306 IPC based on its assessment of the evidence. The Supreme Court is being asked to determine whether the High Court erred in altering the conviction and reducing the sentence.
