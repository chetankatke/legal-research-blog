## Summary
In this appeal, the Supreme Court considered a challenge by A. Savariar, a Junior Assistant in the Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission, against his dismissal from service following departmental proceedings under Rule 17(b) of the Tamil Nadu Civil Services (Classification, Control and Appeal) Rules. The charges against him arose from irregularities in the appointment of invigilators for the 1989-90 Assistant Surgeon competitive examination, most notably that he issued an appointment order to R. Mahalingam, who was on leave, to act as an Invigilator at Bharathiar Arts College for Women without obtaining authorization from the Officer in charge of the Section.
