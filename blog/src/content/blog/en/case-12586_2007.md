# JUDGMENT Transport Corporation, Trivandrum v. Susamma Thomas

## Citation
- **Court:** Supreme Court of India
- **Date:** 23.07.2009
- **Judges:** _Unknown_
- **Citation:** 

## Summary
This is a reference order by a two-Judge Bench of the Supreme Court (Sinha and Cyriac Joseph, JJ.) referring two common questions to a larger Bench concerning the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988. The first question is whether the multiplier specified in the Second Schedule must be scrupulously applied in all cases, and the second is whether the Act provides any criteria for determining the multiplicand, particularly future prospects. The Bench noted conflicting precedents—some treating the Second Schedule multiplier as a guide in Section 166 cases, while *Shanti Pathak* advocated a lesser multiplier without stating a legal principle. The Bench observed that Section 163A does not mention application of any multiplier, and the Second Schedule's multiplier provisions apply only to disability in non-fatal accidents, not to fatal cases, warranting authoritative resolution by a larger Bench.
