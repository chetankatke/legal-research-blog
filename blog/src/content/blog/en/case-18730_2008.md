## Summary
**Summary:**

In *Omprakash v. Laxminarayan & Ors.*, the Supreme Court of India addressed whether an agreement to sell containing a recital that physical possession was handed over to the purchaser must be stamped as a *conveyance* under Schedule 1A of the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (as substituted by M.P. Act 22 of 1990), or merely as an agreement to sell.
