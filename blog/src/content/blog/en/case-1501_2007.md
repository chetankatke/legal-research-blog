## Summary
This appeal by the State of Madhya Pradesh challenged the High Court's acquittal of the respondents (the deceased Kusum Rani's husband and parents-in-law), who had been convicted by the trial court under Sections 498-A and 302 read with Section 34 IPC for burning the deceased alive after persistent harassment and cruelty. The legal question before the Supreme Court was whether the High Court was justified in disregarding the dying declaration recorded by the Executive Magistrate, corroborated by other evidence, and acquitting the accused.
