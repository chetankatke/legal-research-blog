## Summary
This Supreme Court reference opinion, reported as *P. Sudhakar Rao v. U. Govinda Rao*, addresses whether the years of service rendered by a Supervisor (diploma-holder) can be counted as weightage to fix his seniority upon his later designation as a Junior Engineer (degree-holder), thereby granting retrospective seniority. The court drew a key distinction between weightage for promotion eligibility and weightage for seniority, framing the legal question as whether the earlier decisions in *Devi Prasad* and *K.S. Muralidhar* or the later decisions in *G.S. Venkat Reddy*, *K. Narayanan*, and *C.G. Desai* laid down the correct law.
