## Key Holdings

- The Presidential reference under Article 143(1) was held not to be an effective alternative dispute resolution mechanism, and Section 4(3) of the Acquisition of Certain Area at Ayodhya Act, 1993 was declared unconstitutional as it did not cover the core questions in the pending suits.
