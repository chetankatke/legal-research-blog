## Summary
**Summary:**

This Supreme Court appeal arises from a challenge by Rajasthan State Road Transport Corporation to a Division Bench order of the Rajasthan High Court that upheld the quashing of the compulsory retirement of its employee, Babu Lal Jangir, who had joined as a Driver in 1977. The legal question centers on the validity of the respondent's compulsory retirement under amended Rule 18-D(1) of the Rajasthan State Road Transport Workers and Workshop Employees Standing Orders, 1965, which grants the Corporation the absolute right to retire any employee upon attaining the age of 50 or completing 25 years of service, whichever is earlier,
