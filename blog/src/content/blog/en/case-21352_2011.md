## Summary
This Supreme Court case involves Tata Iron & Steel Co. Ltd. challenging a judgment related to its 1999 Business Transfer Agreement with Lafarge India Pvt. Ltd., under which its cement division was hived off. The legal question centers on the rights of employees transferred under Section 25 FF of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, and whether their retrenchment compensation liability passed to Lafarge along with the transfer. The respondents (workers) alleged they were forced to join Lafarge without consent under the false impression that the transfer was temporary.
