## Summary
**Summary:**

This case involves cross-appeals arising from a murder conviction under Section 302 read with Section 34 IPC for the death of Trilokchand on 16.02.1999, following a dispute over a well between the parties. The Trial Court convicted both accused (A-1 Shri Ram and A-2 Shobha Ram) based primarily on the testimony of PW-6, who stated A-1 assaulted the deceased with stones
