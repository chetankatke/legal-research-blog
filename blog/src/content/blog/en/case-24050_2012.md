## Summary

## Key Holdings
- The application for deletion of the names of Respondent Nos. 4 to 8 was allowed as a preliminary matter.
- The appeal was disposed of by directing the High Court to decide the writ appeal on a priority basis, preferably by 7th February 2013, in light of the upcoming elections and acute shortage of police personnel in Tripura.
- The interim order passed by the Division Bench of the High Court was set aside.
- The Supreme Court clarified that the disposal was without adjudicating the merits of the issues involved.
- The order of the learned Single Judge was also directed not to be given effect to until the High Court decided the appeal.
