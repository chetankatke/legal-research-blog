## Summary
**Summary:**

This Supreme Court appeal by the State of U.P. challenges a High Court decision that quashed the State's order withdrawing the Public Distribution System (PDS) from Gram Panchayats and reinstating the earlier policy vesting licensing power in the District Magistrate. The core legal question concerns whether the State Government had the authority to withdraw PDS distribution powers from Gram Panchayats (conferred under U.P.
