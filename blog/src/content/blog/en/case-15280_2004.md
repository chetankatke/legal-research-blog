## Summary
The Supreme Court heard a civil appeal arising from the Andhra Pradesh High Court's decision upholding a civil suit where the respondent claimed title over land originally belonging to the Forest Department, leased in 1943 to M/s. A. Allauddin & Sons with express prohibitions on subletting and structural extensions. The original lessee violated these terms by executing a sub-lease in 1958 and subsequently a sale deed in 1959 in favour of the respondent, despite repeated eviction notices from the State.
