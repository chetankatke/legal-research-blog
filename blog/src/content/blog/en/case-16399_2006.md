## Summary
**Summary:**

This is a criminal appeal by the State of Rajasthan against the High Court of Rajasthan's judgment in a case involving the gang rape and murder of a woman named Rekha by three accused—Ram Niwas, Balveer, and Rajesh—on 1 November 2003. The incident occurred after the accused brought the victim to a forest area near a railway track, where they allegedly committed sexual assault and strangulated her. Rajesh, one of the accused, was granted pardon and made an approver under Section 306 CrPC by the Chief Judicial Magistrate on the ground that he was the sole eyewitness, and the case was registered under Sections 376 and 302 read with Section 34 IPC.
