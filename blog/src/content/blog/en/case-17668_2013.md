## Summary
The Supreme Court disposed of an appeal arising from a winding-up petition filed by the respondent against the appellant company for non-payment of dues of Rs. 13 lakhs (out of Rs. 15 lakhs borrowed), which had been admitted by the Calcutta High Court. During the pendency of the appeal, the parties amicably resolved their dispute, and the appellant had already deposited the Rs. 13 lakhs with the High Court pursuant to the Court's earlier direction. The Court directed that the respondent be permitted to withdraw Rs. 10 lakhs from the deposited amount while the remaining Rs. 3 lakhs would be returned to the appellant, the winding-up proceedings be dropped, and the connected civil suit be expedited for early
