## Summary
**Summary:**

The State of Rajasthan appealed to the Supreme Court against a High Court order that allowed a police officer's writ petition seeking promotion to the senior scale, which had been denied due to a 1992 censure punishment. The legal question centered on whether promotion could be deferred on the basis of a minor punishment (censure) and the proper weightage between seniority and merit in promotions under the Rajasthan Police Service Rules, 1954. The High Court had held, relying on *B.V. Sivaiah v. K. Addanki Babu*, that seniority must be given greater weightage than merit, quashing the denial of promotion.
