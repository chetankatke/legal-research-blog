## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal before the Supreme Court arose from a Rajasthan High Court judgment that allowed writ petitions filed by Diamond and Gem Development Corporation Ltd. quashing RIICO's cancellation of land allotment and directing the provision of an approach/access road to the allotted premises. The core legal question concerned the validity of the cancellation of an industrial land allotment where the allottee-company had failed to complete the project within the stipulated five-year period under the lease deed executed on an "as is-where-is" basis, while also seeking to sub-lease portions of the property. The dispute involved land originally acquired under the Rajasthan Land Acquisition Act, 1953 for industrial development and allotted to the respondent for establishing a Gem Industrial Estate, with RIICO contending that the company's slow development and breach of lease conditions justified cancellation of the allotment.
