**Summary:**

This batch of civil appeals before the Supreme Court of India, decided by Justice G.S. Singhvi, arises from the acquisition of large tracts of land by the Government of Haryana under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894, for
## Key Holdings
- The case involves appeals against enhancement of land acquisition compensation by the Punjab and Haryana High Court from Rs.28,15,356 per acre to Rs.37,40,000 per acre.
- The acquisition was made under the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 by the Government of Haryana for establishing the Industrial Model Township at Manesar (IMT Manesar), District Gurgaon.
- Both the beneficiary of acquisition, Haryana State Industrial and Infrastructure Development Corporation (HSIIDC), and the landowners challenged the enhancement of compensation.
- The Supreme Court granted leave to appeal in the civil appeals arising out of multiple SLPs related to the same acquisition proceedings.
