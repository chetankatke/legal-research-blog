## Summary
This case involves an appeal by Deepak Gulati challenging his conviction by the Punjab & Haryana High Court for offences under Sections 365 (kidnapping) and 376 (rape) of the IPC, arising from an incident on 10.5.1995 where he induced a 19-year-old prosecutrix to accompany him from Karnal to Kurukshetra under the pretext of marriage and subsequently had sexual intercourse with her against her will. The prosecution examined 13 witnesses, and both the Sessions Court, Karnal, and the High Court upheld the conviction, sentencing him to 3 years' RI for kidnapping and 7 years' RI for rape (concurrent), along with fines.
