## Summary
**Summary:**

This appeal before the Supreme Court of India challenges the High Court of Punjab and Haryana's judgment confirming the conviction of Rakesh (husband) and Ram Piari (mother-in-law) under Sections 498-A and 302 read with Section 34 of the IPC for a dowry-related death. The prosecution's case was that the deceased, Kailash, was harassed for dowry and on 14.05.1998 was beaten, doused with kerosene, and set ablaze by the appellants, leading to her death.
